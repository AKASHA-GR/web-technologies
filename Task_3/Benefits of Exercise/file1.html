1. Aerobic Exercise (Cardio)

Exercise that improves heart and lung health

What is Aerobic Exercise?

Aerobic exercise is physical activity that increases your heart rate and breathing for a sustained period of time.

It helps improve cardiovascular fitness and overall health.

Examples

  • Running
  • Swimming
  • Walking
  • Cycling
  • Dancing

Benefits

  • Improves heart health
  • Burns calories
  • Increases stamina
  • Helps control weight
  • Improves mood

Example Activity


Activity: Running
Duration: 30 minutes
Calories Burned: 200-300 kcal
