3. Flexibility Exercises

Exercises that improve joint and muscle movement

What are Flexibility Exercises?

Flexibility exercises help improve the range of motion of joints and muscles.

They are important for maintaining mobility and preventing injuries.

Examples

  • Stretching
  • Yoga
  • Pilates
  • Toe-touch stretches

Benefits

  • Improves flexibility
  • Prevents injuries
  • Improves posture
  • Reduces muscle tension
