About Wipro

Wipro Limited is a global technology services and consulting company headquartered in Bengaluru, India.

Founded in 1945, Wipro started as a consumer products company and later transformed into one of India’s leading IT service providers.

Wipro supports organizations worldwide with consulting, engineering, cloud computing, and AI-based technology solutions.

"Wipro helps companies achieve digital transformation through innovation."

Key Facts

  • Founded: 1945
  • Founder: M. H. Hasham Premji
  • Headquarters: Bengaluru, India
  • Industry: Information Technology Services
  • CEO: Thierry Delaporte
