About Zara

Zara is a Spanish fast-fashion clothing brand owned by the global retail group Inditex.

It was founded in 1975 by Amancio Ortega and Rosalía Mera in La Coruña, Spain.

Zara is known for its fast fashion strategy which quickly converts fashion trends into ready-to-wear clothing.

"Fashion trends change quickly, and Zara delivers them faster."

Key Facts

  • Founded: 1975, La Coruña, Spain
  • Headquarters: Arteixo, Galicia, Spain
  • Parent company: Inditex Group
  • Chairperson: Marta Ortega Pérez
  • CEO: Óscar García Maceiras
  • Stores: 6500+ in 90+ countries
