About Allen Solly

Allen Solly is an Indian clothing and lifestyle brand known for introducing smart-casual fashion in India.

The brand is owned by Aditya Birla Fashion and Retail Limited and was launched in India in 1993.

“Allen Solly popularized the concept of Friday Dressing in India.”

Key Facts

  • Founded: 1993 (India launch)
  • Parent company: Aditya Birla Fashion and Retail Limited
  • Headquarters: Bengaluru, India
  • Product range: Apparel, footwear, accessories
  • Market segment: Premium casual wear
