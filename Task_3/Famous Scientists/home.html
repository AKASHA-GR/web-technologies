What is a Scientist?

  • A scientist studies the natural world using experiments and observation.
  • A scientist discovers new knowledge about nature and technology.

Example quote: Science is a way of thinking. - Carl Sagan

What is an Invention?

  • An invention is something new created for the first time.
  • A device, method, or product designed by humans.

Examples

  1. Light bulb by Thomas Edison
  2. Telephone by Alexander Graham Bell

What is Discovery?

  • Finding something that already existed.
  • Example: Gravity discovered by Isaac Newton.
