Amazon website

Amazon.com, Inc. is a leading global technology company founded by Jeff Bezos in 1994, originally as an online bookstore, and is now a titan in e-commerce, cloud computing (AWS), digital streaming, and artificial intelligence. Headquartered in Seattle, it is one of the world's most valuable companies, operating as "The Everything Store" with over 310 million active users.

Founder & History

Founded in 1994 by Jeff Bezos in Bellevue, Washington, it began as an online bookstore before expanding rapidly.

Business Segments

1.E-commerce: The world's largest online marketplace.
2.Amazon Web Services (AWS): A dominant provider of cloud infrastructure services.
3.Devices & Services: Produces Kindle e-readers, Fire TV, and Echo/Alexa devices.
4.Entertainment & Others: Includes Prime Video, Amazon Music, Audible, and Whole Foods Market.

Key Statistics

1.Market Value: Worth over $2.166 trillion. 2.Prime Users: Over 200 million global members. 3.Website Traffic: Approximately 2.2–2.6 billion visits per month. 4.Employees: Over 600,000.

Philosophy

Driven by "customer obsession," passion for invention, and long-term thinking.

Logistics & Innovation

Operates extensive, automated fulfillment centers and is developing drone delivery via Prime Air.
