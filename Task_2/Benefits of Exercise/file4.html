4. Balance Exercises

Exercises that improve body stability and coordination

What are Balance Exercises?

Balance exercises help improve body stability and coordination.

They are especially important for preventing falls and improving posture.

Examples

  • Tai Chi
  • Standing on one leg
  • Balance board exercises
  • Heel-to-toe walking

Benefits

  • Prevents falls
  • Improves body control
  • Enhances coordination
  • Strengthens core muscles
