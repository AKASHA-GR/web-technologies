2. Strength Training (Anaerobic Exercise)

Exercises that build muscle strength

What is Strength Training?

Strength training is a type of exercise that focuses on building muscle strength and endurance.

It usually involves lifting weights, using resistance bands, or performing body-weight exercises.

Examples

  • Weight lifting
  • Push-ups
  • Squats
  • Resistance band training
  • Pull-ups

Benefits

  • Builds muscles
  • Improves bone strength
  • Increases metabolism
  • Improves body balance
  • Supports joint health

Example Exercise


Exercise: Push-ups
Repetitions: 10–15
Sets: 3
Rest Time: 30 seconds
