LIST OF FAMOUSE SCIENTISTS

What is a scientist?

  • A scientist is a person who studies and researches the natural world using experiments, observation, and analysis to gain knowledge.
  • A scientist is someone who studies, experiments, and discovers new knowledge about nature or technology.

Examples:

  1. Albert Einstein – studied physics and developed the Theory of Relativity.
  2. Isaac Newton – discovered the law of gravity.

What is invention?

  • An invention is something new that a person creates or designs for the first time.
  • An invention is a new product, device, or method created by a person.

Examples:

  1. The light bulb invented by Thomas Edison
  2. The telephone invented by Alexander Graham Bell

What is Discovery?

  • A discovery is finding something that already existed but was not known before.
  • A discovery is finding something that already exists in nature but was unknown earlier.

Examples:

  1. Gravity discovered by Isaac Newton
  2. Penicillin discovered by Alexander Fleming
