About Infosys

Infosys is a leading Indian multinational information technology company headquartered in Bengaluru, Karnataka, India.

The company provides services in digital transformation, software development, business consulting, and outsourcing.

Infosys has played a major role in making India a global technology hub.

"Infosys is one of India's largest and most influential IT companies."

  • Founders: N. R. Narayana Murthy and team
