About TCS

Tata Consultancy Services (TCS) is a leading Indian multinational IT services and consulting company.

It is part of the Tata Group and is headquartered in Mumbai, India.

TCS provides services such as software development, consulting, cloud computing, and business solutions to companies worldwide.

"TCS is one of the largest IT services companies in the world."
