About Gucci

Gucci is an Italian luxury fashion brand founded in 1921 in Florence, Italy by Guccio Gucci.

The brand is famous for combining traditional craftsmanship with modern design.

“Luxury must be comfortable, otherwise it is not luxury.”

Key Facts

  • Founded: 1921, Florence, Italy
  • Founder: Guccio Gucci
  • Parent company: Kering (since 1999)
  • Headquarters: Casellina di Scandicci, Florence
  • Creative director: Demna (appointed 2025)
