About Nike

Nike is a global sportswear and footwear brand owned by Nike, Inc., headquartered in Beaverton, Oregon, United States.

The company was founded in 1964 as Blue Ribbon Sports and later renamed Nike in 1971.

The name comes from the Greek goddess Nike.

“Just Do It”

Key Facts

  • Founded: 1964 (as Blue Ribbon Sports)
  • Headquarters: Beaverton, Oregon, U.S.
  • CEO (2025): John Donahoe
  • Annual revenue: about $46 billion
  • Major brands: Nike, Jordan Brand, Converse
